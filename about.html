Introducing AWS Cloud Club Istanbul Nishtashi University

Learn, Meet, Inspire

Our cloud club gladly welcomes everyone

AWS Cloud Club will give students hands-on experience through projects in the AWS cloud, allowing students to develop both technical and business expertise in the cloud. This club will provide students with industry skills currently in high demand.
We are proud to have more than 180 members who share their knowledge and experiences with one another. We are grateful for the support of our distinguished sponsors, who help make our events possible. If you're interested in partnering with us for one of our future sessions, we'd be happy to hear from you and work together to create a meaningful and inspiring event.
