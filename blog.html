  • January 11, 2024 Post


    January 11, 2024: AWS Cloud Club at Istanbul Nisantasi University recently hosted an enlightening event titled “Your Journey to A Career in the Cloud.” The speaker was Curtis Evans, shared his valuable expertise and insights on carving out a career in the ever-evolving field of cloud computing. The event saw a significant turnout, both virtually and physically. A remarkable 139 students joined the event online, demonstrating the topic’s broad appeal and engagement. In addition, 14 students were present at the event, contributing to a lively gathering. Curtis Evans, offered practical advice, industry viewpoints, and personal experiences related to building a career in the cloud. The diverse audience, which included both online and in-person attendees, facilitated a comprehensive exchange of ideas and provided networking opportunities for the students. We would like to express our gratitude to Curtis Evans for his time, expertise, and engaging presentation. Sincere expression of gratitude to Nesibe Manav Mutlu, Ph.D. for her generous time, effort, and welcoming us into her class.
    Zyad Abdelhafiz
    January 11, 2024
